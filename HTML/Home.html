About Me

I’ve always been fascinated by how things move, think, and work together, which is why I’m pursuing a Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering (Honours) with Mathematical and Computer Sciences, aiming to major in Mechatronics and Robotics. The challenge of turning complex ideas into real-world solutions excites me, and I thrive in hands-on projects where innovation meets practicality.

I have hands-on experience in robotics, CAD, and system integration, gained through various projects and team collaborations. Being part of innovative engineering initiatives, including the Adelaide Rover Team, has allowed me to develop both my technical and teamwork skills, while strengthening my curiosity and drive to keep learning.

Beyond robotics, I’m driven by a curiosity for the unknown, a love for problem-solving, and a desire to contribute to cutting-edge engineering projects. I enjoy mentoring, sharing knowledge with peers, and constantly learning new skills to stay at the forefront of mechatronics and intelligent systems. My goal is to combine mechanical design, programming, and innovation to tackle real-world challenges and push the boundaries of what’s possible.
